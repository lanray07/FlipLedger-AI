# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

FlipLedger AI is a reseller inventory, sales, profit, and listing assistant app.

## Data Stored On Your Device

FlipLedger AI stores inventory items, item photos, purchase prices, sales records, listing drafts, marketplace fee settings, subscription state, notes, and preferences locally on your device using Apple's local storage frameworks.

## Data Collection

The current app build uses mock AI by default and does not transmit your inventory, sales records, photos, or listing drafts to FlipLedger AI servers.

If a future version enables the remote AI backend, item details and selected photos may be sent to FlipLedger AI's secure backend only when you request AI listing, pricing, photo analysis, selling tips, or summary features. API keys must never be stored inside the iOS app.

## Payments

Subscriptions are handled by Apple's StoreKit and App Store payment systems. FlipLedger AI does not receive or store your payment card details.

## Tracking And Advertising

FlipLedger AI does not use the advertising identifier, does not track users across apps or websites, and does not include advertising SDKs in the current build.

## Data Sharing

The current build does not sell, share, or disclose personal data to advertising networks or data brokers.

## User Control

You can export reports from the app and delete all local data from Settings. Deleting local data removes inventory, item photos, sales records, listing drafts, marketplace settings, and subscription cache records stored by the app on your device.

## Estimates And Advice

Profit calculations, fee estimates, AI listing drafts, and pricing suggestions are estimates only. FlipLedger AI is not tax, legal, or financial advice. Users should verify marketplace fees, comparable listings, shipping costs, and local tax obligations.
