# FlipLedger AI Support

For help with FlipLedger AI, use the GitHub repository:

https://github.com/lanray07/FlipLedger-AI

## Common Help Topics

- Add inventory items with purchase cost, target price, source, storage location, photos, and notes.
- Record sales with marketplace, sale price, shipping, platform fee, packaging, promotion, discount, and final profit.
- Use the profit calculator before listing or accepting offers.
- Review AI listing drafts and pricing suggestions before using them.
- Export CSV and PDF reports from the Reports screen.
- Delete all local data from Settings.

## Important

Profit calculations are estimates. Marketplace fees can change. Suggested prices are not guaranteed. FlipLedger AI is not tax, legal, or financial advice.
